Keepers' Chit-Chat

Learn more about wildlife from the keepers. In this interactive session, hear interesting quirks of the animals from our keepers.

Feed the Animals

Feel the excitement as the animals eat out of your hand. You can purchase a portion of food on-site. Feed the elephants, Giraffes, Goats & White Rhinoceros.

Splash Safari

A wet and wild time awaits at this action packed show starring the amazing sea lion. The high-energy antics of our mischievous California sea lion are sure to make a splash!

Animal Friends

Get charmed by the display of special bonds between the domesticated animals and their trainers. With plenty of laughs guaranteed. Definite must to visit!

Elephant Presentation

The Elephant Presentation showcases how magnificent these megaherbivores are. Come find out how intelligent, sociable, strong and yet gentle elephants really are!
